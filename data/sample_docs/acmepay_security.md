# AcmePay — Security and Account Access

## Two-factor authentication
Two-factor authentication (2FA) is required on every AcmePay account. You can use
an authenticator app or SMS codes. Authenticator apps are recommended because SMS
can be intercepted.

## Resetting your password
To reset your password, go to Settings > Security > Reset password. You will need
access to your registered email and must confirm a code sent to your 2FA method.
Password resets sign you out of all devices.

## Card controls
You can freeze and unfreeze your AcmePay card instantly in the app. A frozen card
declines all new payments but still allows recurring subscriptions you approved.

## Supported countries
AcmePay is available in the United States, the United Kingdom, the United Arab
Emirates, India, and the European Union. Support for Singapore is planned for
2026. AcmePay is not available in countries under sanctions.
