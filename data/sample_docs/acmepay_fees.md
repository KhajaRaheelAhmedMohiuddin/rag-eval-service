# AcmePay — Fees and Limits

AcmePay is a digital wallet for sending, spending, and saving money.

## Account fees
Opening an AcmePay account is free. There is no monthly maintenance fee on the
Standard plan. The Premium plan costs 9.99 USD per month and adds higher limits
and priority support.

## Transfer fees
Domestic transfers between AcmePay users are free and instant. Bank withdrawals
to a linked account are free and take 1 to 2 business days. Instant bank
withdrawal is available for a 1.5% fee, capped at 10 USD.

## Currency exchange
Currency conversion uses the mid-market rate plus a 0.5% margin on the Standard
plan. Premium plan users get the mid-market rate with no margin on the first
5,000 USD converted each month.

## Limits
Standard accounts can hold up to 20,000 USD and send up to 10,000 USD per day.
Premium accounts can hold up to 100,000 USD and send up to 50,000 USD per day.
