# AcmePay — Refunds and Disputes

## Requesting a refund
If a payment to a merchant was made in error, you can request a refund from the
merchant directly in the app under Activity > Transaction > Request refund.
Merchant refunds are typically processed within 5 to 10 business days.

## Disputes and chargebacks
If a merchant does not respond within 10 business days, you can open a dispute.
AcmePay reviews disputes within 14 business days. If the dispute is approved, the
amount is returned to your AcmePay balance.

## Failed or cancelled transfers
A transfer that fails is automatically reversed to your balance within 1 business
day. You do not need to contact support for automatic reversals.

## Unauthorised transactions
Report unauthorised transactions within 60 days. Confirmed unauthorised
transactions are fully refunded, and the affected card is reissued.
